# AI Agent Definitions

## Researcher
- **Role**: Discovers and extracts market data from various sources.
- **Tools**: Web Search, Content Extraction.
- **Output**: Raw findings with source URLs.

## Auditor
- **Role**: Verifies the credibility of the sources and the accuracy of the researcher's findings.
- **Process**: Checks each source URL, cross-references facts, and flags inconsistencies.
- **Output**: Verified data points or rejection with reasons.

## Report Generator
- **Role**: Synthesizes verified data into a professional market research report.
- **Format**: Markdown/HTML with executive summary, key findings, and references.
- **Output**: Final Report.
